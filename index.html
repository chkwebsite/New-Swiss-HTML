About Us

The Swiss Agency for Development and Cooperation (SDC) has been a partner of India for more than 60 years. Since 2011, SDC’s engagement focuses specifically on climate change adaptation and mitigation, and other environmental challenges. The office in India is part of SDC’s Global Programme Climate Change and Environment (GPCCE). SDC is working across the Indian Himalayan Region (IHR) in close collaboration with the Ministry of Environment, Forest and Climate Change, National Disaster Management Authority, NITI Aayog, Department of Science and Technology, Ministry of Jal Shakti, State Government Nodal Agencies, National and International partners. SDCs work complements an increased focus of the Government of India on sustainable development of the Himalayan region with Switzerland’s expertise in addressing climate change concerns through systems development and modelling technologies for scalable adaption actions in mountain ecosystems.
